**SERVICE AGREEMENT**

Between: **Curb, operated by [DAD'S FULL LEGAL NAME], sole proprietor** ("Provider") and

**1. Services.** Provider will deliver the package selected below:
- [ ] Website — $1,200 one-time (no contract)
- [ ] Site + Lead Gen Bundle — $500 setup + Lead Gen Launch below
- [ ] Lead Gen Only — $250 setup + Lead Gen Launch below

Lead Gen tier:
- [ ] Starter — 3-month launch $1,080 prepaid, then $450/mo
- [ ] Growth — 3-month launch $1,920 prepaid, then $800/mo
- [ ] Full Funnel — 3-month launch $3,240 prepaid, then $1,350/mo

Tier deliverables are as listed at curb pricing page on the date of signing (attach a copy).

**2. Term.** Lead gen begins with a 3-month launch period, paid in advance (the prepaid price
above reflects a 20% discount for upfront payment). After the launch period, service continues
month-to-month at the regular rate. Either party may cancel after the launch period with 30 days'
written notice. The launch prepayment is non-refundable once work has begun.

**3. Payment.** Payment is by PayPal invoice (card accepted). Setup fees and launch prepayment are due before work begins. Monthly fees are due
on the 1st of each month after the launch period. Late payments pause work.

**4. Ad spend.** Any advertising budget (Google, Meta, or similar) is paid by Client directly to
the platform. Provider never holds or marks up ad spend.

**5. Ownership.** The website, domain, and content belong to Client. Domain and hosting are
registered in Client's name. If service ends, Client keeps everything.

**6. No guarantees.** Provider does not guarantee search rankings, lead volume, or revenue.
Provider commits to performing the listed services diligently and reporting results honestly,
including call-tracking data where applicable.

**7. Client responsibilities.** Client will provide timely access to accounts (Google Business
Profile, etc.), photos, and business information, and will complete any platform verification
(e.g., Google Local Services screening) that only the owner can complete. Delays on these delay
delivery.

**8. Liability.** Provider's total liability is limited to the fees paid in the prior 3 months.
Provider is not liable for platform decisions (Google/Meta suspensions, ranking changes, etc.).

**9. Communication.** Primary channel: email/text. Monthly report delivered by the 5th of each month.
